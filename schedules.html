Compass Making

Materials needed:

  • A leaf
  • A paper clip
  • A strong magnet
  • A small bowl of water

Instructions:

  1. Magnetize the paper clip: Rub the paper clip repeatedly in one direction along the magnet. Rub it at least 20-30 times to give it a strong magnetic charge.
  2. Float the leaf: Gently place the leaf flat on the surface of the water in the bowl. If it sinks, try to find a different leaf or a different bowl of water.
  3. Place the paper clip: Carefully lay the magnetized paper clip on top of the floating leaf.
  4. Watch it turn: The paper clip will slowly turn until one of its ends points north, acting like a compass.

Game Instructions

🚢 Ship to Shore Game

Classic movement game with different commands for different actions around the hall.

📣 Beep Beep Game
  • Setup: Beavers get into pairs and decide on a secret signal only they know
  • Play: One partner is blindfolded and spun around
  • Challenge: The seeing partner calls out their secret signal (e.g., "Beep beep")
  • Goal: The blindfolded partner must find their partner using only sound
  • Switch: Then they swap roles

Tea Bag Rocket Experiment

Materials needed:

  • Funnel tea bags
  • Square silver tin foil pieces
  • Lighter (adult use only)

Instructions:

  1. Preparation: Snip both ends of tea bags and remove all tea leaves
  2. Safety: Place the empty tea bag on tin foil to protect the table
  3. Experiment: Light the top of the tea bag and watch it burn down
  4. Magic: As oxygen is used up, the burned bag floats to the ceiling!
  5. Participation: Each Beaver gets their own turn to watch the experiment

Two Truths and One Lie Game

  • Setup: Beavers sit in a circle
  • Turn Taking: One Beaver shares 2 truths and 1 lie about themselves
  • Voting: The group votes on which statement they think is the lie
  • Success: If the majority guesses correctly, move to the next Beaver
  • Continue: Keep going around the circle

🧭 Compass Making

Watch this video to see how it's done:

Materials needed (bring if you can!):

  • 🧲 A magnet
  • � A paper clip
  • 🍷 A cork (a regular wine cork is ideal)
  • A small bowl of water (provided)

Instructions:

  1. Magnetize the paper clip: Rub the paper clip repeatedly in one direction along the magnet. Rub it at least 20–30 times to give it a strong magnetic charge.
  2. Prepare the cork: Cut a thin slice of cork so it floats flat on water.
  3. Float the cork: Gently place the cork slice flat on the surface of the water in the bowl.
  4. Place the paper clip: Carefully lay the magnetized paper clip on top of the floating cork.
  5. Watch it turn: The paper clip will slowly turn until one of its ends points north, acting like a compass!
