Objective

To reinforce the new lodges and introduce the Beaver Scout Law in a fun, competitive way.

Instructions

  • Preparation: Before the meeting, write or print out the words of the Beaver Scout Law on separate slips of paper. You need a complete set of words for each lodge.
  • The Challenge: Give each lodge an envelope containing the jumbled words. Explain that they must work together as a team to arrange the words in the correct order: "A Beaver Scout has fun, and is kind and helpful."
  • The Race: On your command, the lodges begin. The first team to correctly lay out the law and have it checked by a leader wins.
  • Fall In: Once the game is over, gather the Beavers and have them recite the Beaver Scout Law.
