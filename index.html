About Our Beaver Colony

Who We Are

We are the 25th Limerick St. Oliver Plunkett Beaver Scouts, part of Scouting Ireland. Our colony meets every Tuesday night in Shannon Banks, Corbally, Limerick.

What We Do

Beavers enjoy games, crafts, nature activities, and fun adventures. We help children develop confidence, make friends, and learn new skills in a safe and supportive environment.

Age Group

Beaver Scouts is for children aged 6-8 years old. It's the perfect introduction to the world of Scouting with age-appropriate activities and challenges.
